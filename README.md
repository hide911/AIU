# 🤖 AIU - Artificial Intelligence University

A modern, responsive HTML landing page for Artificial Intelligence University. Designed to showcase AI courses, features, and testimonials.

## 📋 Features

- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- ✅ **Modern UI** - Beautiful gradient design with smooth animations
- ✅ **Navigation** - Easy-to-use navigation with smooth scrolling
- ✅ **Hero Section** - Eye-catching landing section with call-to-action button
- ✅ **Features Section** - Highlights why students should choose AIU
- ✅ **Courses Section** - Displays 6 professional AI courses with pricing
- ✅ **About Section** - Information about AIU with statistics
- ✅ **Testimonials** - Student reviews and ratings
- ✅ **Newsletter** - Email subscription form
- ✅ **Contact Section** - Contact information and social links
- ✅ **Interactive Elements** - Hover effects, smooth scrolling, form handling

## 🎨 Design Features

- **Color Scheme**: Purple gradient (#667eea to #764ba2)
- **Typography**: Segoe UI, clean and professional
- **Layout**: CSS Grid and Flexbox for responsive design
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions and hover effects
- **Mobile Optimized**: Fully responsive on all devices

## 🌐 Customization

### Change Colors
Find this line in the CSS:
```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea 0%, #764ba2 100%);
```
Replace `#667eea` and `#764ba2` with your desired colors.

### Update Course Information
Find the courses section in HTML and update:
```html
<h3>Course Name</h3>
<div class="course-level">Level • Duration</div>
<p>Description here</p>
<div class="course-price">$Price</div>
```

### Update Contact Information
Find the contact section and update:
- Email address
- Phone number
- Physical address
- Social media links
